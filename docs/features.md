# Features

## 1. AI Tutor

The AI Tutor helps students understand concepts through personalized explanations.

### Capabilities

* Answer student questions
* Explain concepts in simple language
* Provide examples and step-by-step solutions
* Generate follow-up questions

---

## 2. Study Planner

Creates personalized study schedules based on student goals.

### Capabilities

* Daily study plans
* Weekly schedules
* Goal tracking
* Time management recommendations

---

## 3. Quiz Generator

Generates quizzes to test student understanding.

### Capabilities

* Multiple-choice questions
* Short-answer questions
* Topic-based quizzes
* Instant feedback

---

## 4. Progress Tracking

Tracks learning progress and study consistency.

### Capabilities

* Learning streaks
* Completed topics
* Study time tracking
* Performance reports

---

## 5. Learning Analytics

Identifies strengths and weaknesses.

### Capabilities

* Weak topic detection
* Strong topic identification
* Progress visualization
* Improvement suggestions

---

## 6. Personalized Recommendations

Provides learning recommendations based on student performance.

### Capabilities

* Recommended topics
* Revision suggestions
* Practice exercises
* Learning path guidance
